About Me


I'm a final year student at the University of Wollonong in Australia studying Computer Science. I recently completed a 7-months internship with Twenty Billion Neurons as an Associate Product Management Intern where I successfully launched Fitness Ally on the iOS app store. I've also interned at Rolls-Royce Digital Solutions and Mizzou Publishing. Check out my latest work!

I previously attended the University of Missouri, Columbia to study Bachelor of Journalism and International Business (2 years) where I was the vice-president for UNICEF USA Campus club, and represented the state of Missouri at the "2017 UNICEF Annual Meeting" on the Capitol Hill in Washington D.C.

Growing up, studying and working in contact with such different cultures in UAE, USA, Germany, and Australia has created in me a real sense of importance of diversity and curiosity to learn. Outside of school and work, I enjoy traveling, cooking and bollywood dancing.
